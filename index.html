Card Generator

Our unique Card generating system empowers you to manage your ID card project easily & successfully/ It offers a wholistic dashboard where you can
manage every aspect of the process from data capturing to printing. You can even manage the delivery of your printed cards to multiple addresses and
track delivery using the same dashboard. The card generating system is cloud-based thus always on and accessible from any device anywhere. It doesnt
require any special equipment. It comes free with each project.
The card generator platform enables you to:
  • Upload data from your pre-existing database to generate the cards
  • Preview generated cards to edit before printing
  • Print cards & manage entire printing process remotely
  • Insert single or multiple address to have card delivered anywhere in the country
  • No IT knowledge required, its designed to be simple for anyone to use
